About the Contest

Shine.com Search ke Sikander, is a contest to find Delhi/NCR’s best and fastest recruiters. This contest gives you an opportunity to show how good you are at searching the most relevant candidate and win yourself cash reward* every day. The contest will start in June and a winner would be announced every day. Login now to participate.

How to participate

1. Register & Login

Give a missed call on 07842867514. Log on to contest.shine.com, after you receive login details

2. Search

A new job description would be displayed everyday at 10 am. Be the fastest one to search the most relevant candidate on Shine.com mini

3. Win Cash Reward

Send in your entry at contest@shine.com and stand a chance to win cash reward of Rs. 10,000* everyday

4. Come again, Play again

Log on to contest.shine.com next day at 09:30 am to check if you are a winner. If not, don't give up. Play again!

About Shine Mini

Shine.com is a concise version of Shine.com and has limited database as compared to Shine.com’s vast candidate database. It is used only for the purpose of demo and let users participate in the contest.
