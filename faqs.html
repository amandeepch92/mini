Frequently Asked Questions

What is Search Ke Sikander?
Shine.com Search ke Sikander, is a contest to find Delhi/NCR’s best and fastest recruiters. This contest gives you an opportunity to show how good you are at searching the most relevant candidate and win yourself cash reward* every day. The contest will start in June 2016 and a winner would be announced every day.

How can I Register?
Give a missed call on 07842867514 or Log on to contest.shine.com

Do I need to pay any charges/fees to participate?
There is no entry fee for participation in the contest & Shine.com does not authorize anybody to charge any fee for this contest.

My friend is participating. Can I also participate?
Yes, any recruiter can participate. Only information required is:

  • Name
  • Email-id
  • Mobile No.
  • Company Name

I haven’t registered yet, but the contest has already started. Can I participate now?
You can register by just giving the missed call on 07842867514
After you receive login details → Log on to contest.shine.com & start playing!

What is the duration of the contest?
Contest will be held everyday from starting 15th June till 14th July.
Contest Time: 10:00 am – 2:00 pm

How many winners will be announced everyday & how will I know that I am the Winner?
There will be only one winner each day.
Don’t forget to login to check for your name everyday @ 9:30 am on contest@shine.com
You could be a winner.

Where should I submit my entry every day?
Send in your entry at contest@shine.com and stand a chance to win cash reward of Rs. 10,000* everyday

What will happen in case of a tie?
Recruiter who will send the best/relevant resume in shortest time will be declared as a winner. T&C apply.

If I have already won the contest, can I win again if I participate? OR Can a participant win this contest twice or thrice?
Yes, You can win multiple times. So keep participating.
